2. The "Instant" Mains Evaluator

Getting feedback on Mains answers is slow, expensive, and inconsistent. Our platform gives you an AI-powered co-pilot to grade your answers instantly.

Instant AI Feedback: Submit your handwritten or typed answers for any Mains question (PYQ or custom). Our AI will provide a detailed evaluation in seconds, analyzing your structure, clarity, introduction, conclusion, and how well you addressed the question's demands.

Build a Personal Archive of Your Progress: We don't just grade your answer and forget it. We save every single evaluation in your private, searchable archive.

Track Your Improvement: Your archive is not a "junk drawer"; it's a powerful tool. You can instantly filter and find all your past answers for a specific topic.

"Show me all my evaluated answers for 'GS2 - Governance'."
